Ottawa's Mobile Bike Mechanic (OMBM) is a small company run by Austin Chamney, a mechanic with experience at re-Cycles community bike shop.

OMBM's goal is to provide professional mechanical service in a convienent and affordable manner. The hope is that this will encourage fellow Ottawa citizens to get outside and enjoy the fresh air.

Services

  • Hourly mechanical service. $40 for the first hour and $20 thereafter:
    • Tune ups
    • Repairs
    • Safety check
    • Replace flat tires/tubes
    • Build bicycles
    • Replace worn drive train
    • Diagnose and fix problems
  • We buy your old bikes from you. If you are looking to get rid of your old steed and don't want to pay to fix it up, we can buy it from you instead.
